##SPELLING RULES

We use the American spelling 
'We use single parenthesis'


###Start of sentence*:

* Ebook
* Ereader
* iBook
* Epubreader
* EPUBreader (Firefox add-on)

*exception: E-ink

-- avoid 'epub' if refering to the general kind of electronic publication and instead use *E-publication(s)* if possible. 


###In the sentence*:

* ebook
* ereader
* ebookstore
* iBook

*exception: e-ink

-- avoid 'epub' if refering to the general kind of electronic publication and instead use *e-publication(s)* if possible.
